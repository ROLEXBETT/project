Our Services

Hybrid Cars

We offer top-level services for hybrid vehicles. We are at the forefront of providing specialized services for all hybrid cars on Kenyan roads. Our diagnostic systems are from Germany manufacturer and efficient and effective to facilitate vehicle analysis and troubleshooting. The system can read virtually all cars on the Kenyan roads. This enables us to restore vehicle’s performance to manufacturer’s specification.
