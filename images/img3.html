One stop shop for all your vehicle needs
Japanese Vehicle Service Centre (JVSC) is a comprehensive garage providing a one stop shop for all vehicle owners. Our service centers are fully equipped with state-of-the art equipment for all your vehicle needs. Our professional team is on hand to offer superior and timely services.
Services – “Beyond the Standard”
Our strength lies in our commitment to high ethical standards and a friendly attitude. We aim to earn the customer’s trust from the moment they walk through our door.
Modern Management System
JVSC has invested in a modern garage management systems that captures, records and maintains detailed information of every vehicle that comes in for any services in their database which ensures proper diagnosis, maintenance and future references of work done at any given time.
Experienced Technicians
The crew comprises of efficient and highly trained technicians that are well outfitted with the latest automotive understanding and believe in providing exceptional auto-service quality to our customers.
